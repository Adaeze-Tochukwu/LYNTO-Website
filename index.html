How it Works

Up and running in three steps.

No lengthy onboarding. No complex setup. Lynto is designed to fit how your agency already works — and make it smarter.

1

Agency Sets Up

Register your agency, add your carers and clients, configure care plans and alert thresholds — all in minutes from the admin panel. Each client gets their own profile with assigned carers and a full history log.

2

Carers Log in the Field

Carers use the Lynto mobile app to check in on arrival, complete guided patient assessments, record observations and medications — and submit visits on the go, even with poor connectivity.

3

Managers Monitor Everything

Receive instant amber and red alerts the moment a concern is flagged. View the live dashboard for real-time status across all patients, carers, and active visits — anywhere, anytime.
